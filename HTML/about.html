ABOUT US

Established in 1976, our 49er Men's Soccer Club is a competitive division I program known for its dedication to discipline and excellence. With multiple NCAA tournament appearances, our program has the reputation of developing the next generation of talent. Our team plays our home games on our Transamerica Field which opened in 1996.
